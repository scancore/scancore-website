Technology

How LiDAR scanning actually works

Light Detection and Ranging

Our scanners emit millions of laser pulses per second, measuring the return time of each to create a precise 3D map of every surface — walls, ceilings, floors, built-ins — in minutes.

LiDAR Precision You Can Build With

Unlike tape measures or photogrammetry, LiDAR captures millions of verified data points simultaneously. Every wall angle, ceiling height, and fixture position is measured — not estimated. Your deliverables reflect reality, not approximations.

From Raw Scan to Ready-to-Use Files

Our certified team converts raw scan data into clean, layered, organized deliverables. Production-ready files you can open and immediately start designing with — properly named, logically organized, and built for your software.

1–4 Hours On-Site

A typical residential scan takes 1–4 hours. No scaffolding, no specialized access — just a technician with a handheld or tripod-mounted scanner.
